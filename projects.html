Gold Player

See on GitHub
Uses C# and Unity

My personal favorite project! Gold Player is an advanced yet simple to use player controller for Unity. It's the result of years of trying to make a simple player controller. I've used it myself for 3 games, Jumps (when Gold Player was in early development), Santa Gets A Shotgun, and Santa Gets A Tower. To me, it feels production ready!
